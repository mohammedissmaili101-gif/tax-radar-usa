Freelance Taxes in Arkansas (2026)

Freelancers in Arkansas pay top rate of 4.4% in state income tax on top of federal taxes.

As a freelancer in Arkansas, you are responsible for paying both the employee and employer portions of Social Security and Medicare taxes, known as the self-employment tax, which is 15.3% on net earnings.

The federal income tax rate for freelancers depends on your total income bracket. TaxRadar uses a blended estimate of 15% federal rate for accurate forecasting.

Tax Tip: In Arkansas, consider contributing to a SEP-IRA or Solo 401(k) to reduce your taxable income and lower your state and federal tax burden.
