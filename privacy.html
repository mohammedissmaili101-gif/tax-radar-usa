Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At TaxRadar, we take your financial privacy seriously. Unlike other tax tools:

  • No Personal Data: We do not ask for your name, email, or SSN.
  • Client-Side Calculation: All calculations are performed in your browser. Your income data never touches our servers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use invasive tracking cookies.

We use Google AdSense to serve ads, which may use non-personalized cookies to provide a better user experience.
