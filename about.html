About TaxRadar

TaxRadar was built with one goal: Transparency.

For too long, US freelancers and digital nomads have struggled with complex, bloated tax calculators that require sign-ups and personal data. We decided to change that.

Our engine provides high-precision tax forecasting for 2026, specifically optimized for high-income contractors in California, New York, Texas, and Florida. We believe that knowing your "Take-home pay" should be fast, free, and private.

Powered by the Focushive Analytics Engine, we ensure our logic remains up-to-date with the latest IRS and State tax brackets.
